What to Expect

What it's like
in session

You don't have to come in knowing what to say or how to say it. Sessions are conversational by nature, there's no agenda you need to prepare for. Aly will follow your lead and help you make sense of what's coming up. No single modality fits everyone. The work is tailored to you, not the other way around.

Most clients start with weekly or bi-weekly sessions. The work moves at a pace that's right for you, not on a fixed timeline.
